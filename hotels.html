— Introduction —

A trusted operating partner for hospitality.

For more than four decades, Pico Cleaners has served the laundry, dry-cleaning, and textile needs of Los Angeles' leading hotels, luxury venues, restaurants, and entertainment organizations. Today we operate four facilities — including a dedicated hotel-processing center in Culver City — and partner with over fifty four- and five-star properties across the city.

Our work is invisible by design. What our partners notice is what doesn't happen: no missed deliveries, no compromised garments, no last-minute scrambles. Quiet, consistent, expert service — turn after turn, season after season.

— Services —

What we handle for hospitality partners.

From the moment a guest hands over their suit to the moment a ballroom's table linens go up — we cover the full textile lifecycle of a luxury property.

01

Guest garment service

Same-day pressing, dry cleaning, and laundering for guest items. Expert handling of designer clothing, formal wear, and delicates returned with hotel-standard presentation.

02

Uniform care

Consistent processing of staff uniforms — front-of-house, housekeeping, kitchen, valet — at the volume and turnaround a property demands.

03

Linen & tabletop

Sheet, pillowcase, tablecloth, napkin, and banquet linen service. Expert cleaning, fine folding, hand-finished detailing, and specialty stain removal.

04

Wedding & special event

Wedding gown cleaning and preservation. Event linens, evening wear, formal attire — handled with the care a once-in-a-lifetime garment deserves.

05

Drapery cleaning

On-site take-down, processing, and re-hang of fine drapery. Roman shade and Hunter Douglas products handled with the utmost care.

06

Furniture & upholstery

Expert cleaning of luxury furniture. Carpet cleaning with advanced formulations. Asian and Persian rug cleaning. Reupholstery available for property and hospitality-grade pieces.

07

Custom fabrication

Custom drapery design and fabrication. Hunter Douglas specialist. Roman shade creation. Free estimates for property design and refresh projects.

08

Alterations & tailoring

Professional tailoring and alterations seven days a week. Formal wear, hemming, zippers, sleeves, repairs, and re-sizing. Designer clothing handled with expertise.

09

Comforter & large items

Comforter, bedspread, and blanket cleaning at scale. Equipped for high-volume large-item processing with rapid turnaround.
