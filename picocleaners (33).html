A note from the family

Three generations of garment care on the same block. The neighborhood has changed around us — the standard hasn't. Every piece you bring us is treated like our own.

Forty-five years on Pico Blvd.

Pico Cleaners has been West Los Angeles's neighborhood cleaner since 1980. Family-owned, certified green, and built around the idea that the clothes worth keeping deserve to be cared for like they matter.

We handle daily shirts and special-occasion gowns with the same attention. We press, we mend, we steam, and we tell you the truth about what can — and can't — be saved. Free pickup and delivery throughout the neighborhood. Same-day service when you need it. VIP drive-through when you're in a rush.

From your door, back to your closet.

Four steps. Most of the time you don't even need to be home.

01

Schedule

Book a pickup window online or call us — we offer same-day pickup most days when scheduled before noon.

02

We pick up

Leave your bag at the door. Our driver swings by during your window, scans your tag, and takes everything to the shop.

03

We clean

Each piece is inspected, tagged by hand, and routed to the right station — dry clean, launder, alter, or specialty.

04

We deliver

Pressed, hung, and bagged. Back at your door within 1–3 days depending on the service. Bill us monthly if you prefer.
